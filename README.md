# 🚀 CareerCoach AI – Intelligent Resume & Interview Assistant

> Architecting your professional future with AI

CareerCoach AI is an AI-powered platform designed to help students and job seekers improve their resumes and prepare for interviews through personalized, real-time guidance.

It combines **resume analysis**, **role-based optimization**, and **AI-driven interview simulations** into a single seamless experience.

---

## 🌐 Live Demo

🔗 **App Link:** https://ai.studio/apps/c20a0e2c-b0f8-4596-b35e-e66c3bb774a5?fullscreenApplet=true

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

- Lack of personalized career guidance  
- Generic resume feedback with no ATS focus  
- Limited access to realistic interview practice  
- Expensive or inaccessible coaching platforms  

---

## 💡 Solution

CareerCoach AI provides a **smart, AI-driven career assistant** that:

- Analyzes resumes using AI  
- Suggests improvements based on job roles  
- Conducts mock interviews (text/voice)  
- Provides real-time feedback on performance  

---

## ✨ Features

### 🟣 Resume Analyzer
- AI-based resume evaluation  
- Keyword and structure analysis  
- ATS-friendly suggestions  

---

### 🟢 Interview Simulation Lab
- Role-specific interview generation  
- Dynamic Q&A simulation  
- Voice + text interaction support  

---

### 🔵 Real-Time Feedback
- Instant evaluation of answers  
- Feedback on:
  - Confidence  
  - Communication  
  - Relevance  

---

### 🧑‍💼 Identity & Personalization
- User profile management  
- Personalized recommendations  

---

### 🌐 24/7 AI Career Coach
- Always available guidance  
- No dependency on human mentors  

---

## 🧠 How It Works

1. User logs in and sets up profile  
2. Uploads or pastes resume  
3. AI analyzes and suggests improvements  
4. User selects target job role  
5. Enters Interview Simulation Lab  
6. Receives real-time feedback
